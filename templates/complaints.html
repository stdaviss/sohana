How to Make a Complaint

SOHANA takes all complaints seriously. Here is how to raise one and what to expect from our response.

Get started free How it works
📬

Step 1: Contact our support team

Email support@sohana.app with a clear description of your complaint. Include your account phone number and any relevant dates or transaction references. We will acknowledge your complaint within 24 hours.

⏱️

Step 2: Our investigation

We aim to resolve all complaints within 15 business days. Complex cases may take up to 35 business days. We will keep you informed of progress throughout.

⚖️

Step 3: If you are not satisfied

If our resolution does not satisfy you, you may escalate to the relevant financial ombudsman or regulatory authority in your country of residence. Contact legal@sohana.app for the appropriate escalation path.

📋

What makes a good complaint

The more detail you provide — dates, amounts, screenshots, what you expected versus what happened — the faster we can investigate and resolve your issue.
