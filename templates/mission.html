The problem we are solving

800 million adults in sub-Saharan Africa are unbanked or underbanked. This is not because they do not save. They save through Tontines, Njangis, Esusu, and Chamas — community savings systems with centuries of proven reliability. The problem is that this reliability is invisible to the formal financial system.

🔑

Our solution

SOHANA makes the invisible visible. Every contribution a member makes is recorded. Every on-time payment builds a score. Every circle completed is a credential. We are building the first credit infrastructure designed specifically for community-based financial behaviour.

📅

Ten-year vision

By 2035, we aim to have given 25 million people a verifiable financial identity built entirely from their savings behaviour — and to have used that identity to connect them to loans, insurance, and financial services they could never access before.

🤝

Our values

Community first. Transparency always. Technology in service of people, not the other way around. We build for trust because trust is what our users already bring to the table.
