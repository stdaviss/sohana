Security at SOHANA

SOHANA is built with security as a first principle, not an afterthought. Here is how we protect your money, your data, and your identity.

Get started free How it works
🔐

Your funds are segregated

SOHANA does not hold your money in its own accounts. All user balances are held in segregated accounts at licensed partner institutions. If anything happens to SOHANA, your money is protected.

🛡️

OTP on every outgoing transfer

Every withdrawal, payment, or currency conversion requires a one-time password sent to your registered phone number. No transfer leaves your account without your explicit confirmation.

🔒

End-to-end encryption

All data transmitted between your device and SOHANA servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Your personal and financial data is never stored in plain text.

👁️

AML and fraud monitoring

Every transaction is screened by our internal fraud detection system. Unusual patterns trigger automatic alerts reviewed by our Fraud Analyst team within 24 hours.
