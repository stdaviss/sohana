What cookies we use

SOHANA uses strictly necessary cookies to keep you signed in and protect your session. We use analytics cookies to understand how the platform is used. We do not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data.

Managing your preferences

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling session cookies will require you to sign in again on each visit.

📊

Analytics

We use privacy-preserving analytics to understand which features are most used and where users encounter difficulty. This data is aggregated and never tied to individual identity.
