How SOHANA Works

SOHANA is a platform for community savings, fundraising, and cross-border payments — built for the African diaspora and domestic African markets.

Get started free How it works
1️⃣

Create your account

Register with your phone number or email. Get a unique Hanatag. Open your first currency wallet. The whole process takes under two minutes.

2️⃣

Join or create a savings circle

Browse public circles or create your own. Set the contribution amount, frequency, and payout order. Invite members by phone, Hanatag, or shareable link.

3️⃣

Contribute on time, every time

Each on-time contribution builds your NCS score. The platform tracks everything automatically. Organisers see a live dashboard of who has paid and who hasn't.

4️⃣

Receive your payout

When your turn comes, the pot is transferred instantly to your wallet — minus the 1.25% platform fee. Withdraw to your bank or mobile money whenever you like.
