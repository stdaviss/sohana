Contribution Pools

A Contribution Pool is a permanent collective fund for your group or association — built over time, governed by consensus, and protected by three-admin co-authorisation on every disbursement.

Get started free How it works
🏦

Long-term collective funds

Unlike a ROSCA where the pot rotates, a Contribution Pool is a permanent treasury your group builds over months or years — for funerals, weddings, medical emergencies, or community projects.

✅

Three-admin co-authorisation

No single person can release funds from a Contribution Pool. All disbursements require approval from three designated pool admins, eliminating the risk of misappropriation.

📅

Flexible payment schedules

Members choose how they contribute: monthly, quarterly, bi-annually, or annually. The platform tracks who has paid and who has arrears automatically.

🤝

Pay for another member

When a fellow member is going through hardship, you can cover their contribution on their behalf. This earns you extra NCS points as a recognition of solidarity.
