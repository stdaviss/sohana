How Njangi circles work

A Njangi (also known as Tontine, ROSCA, or Esusu) is a rotating savings group where every member contributes a fixed amount each period. The pooled amount goes to one member at a time until everyone has received it once.

🔒

Built-in trust and governance

Every circle on SOHANA requires organiser approval for new members. Three co-admins must agree on any change to payout order. Your money is protected by rules, not promises.

📊

Your NCS score grows with every circle

Every on-time contribution adds to your Njangi Credit Score. The longer you participate, the stronger your financial identity becomes.

🌍

Cross-border circles

Run a circle with members in France, Cameroon, Rwanda, and the UK simultaneously. Contributions and payouts settle across our multicurrency wallet instantly.
