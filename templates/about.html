About SOHANA

SOHANA is a fintech platform that digitalises community savings and builds credit identities for Africa and the African diaspora.

Get started free How it works
🌍

Our story

SOHANA was founded with one conviction: that the billions of people who participate in community savings groups deserve a financial platform built specifically for them — not an adaptation of one built for someone else. We started in the African diaspora in Europe, where Tontines and Njangis have run for decades in notebooks and WhatsApp groups.

💡

What makes us different

We did not build a bank that happens to support savings groups. We built a savings group platform that happens to have a wallet. The distinction matters. Every feature we have built — from the NCS score to the three-admin disbursement model — comes from studying how community finance actually works, not how Western finance thinks it should work.

🏆

Recognition

SOHANA was selected as a winning project at the Tony Elumelu Foundation Entrepreneurial Award 2026 — recognition of both the innovation and the scale of the opportunity we are addressing.

🎯

Our mission

To make community savings the foundation of financial inclusion for 800 million adults in Africa and the African diaspora who have been excluded from formal financial systems — not because they are unreliable, but because no one was recording their reliability.
