Our commitment

SOHANA is committed to making our platform accessible to all users regardless of disability or assistive technology. We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA across all our web interfaces.

📱

Current accessibility features

The SOHANA web platform supports keyboard navigation, screen readers, and high-contrast display modes. All form fields have appropriate labels. Error messages are descriptive and actionable.

🔧

Known limitations

Some data visualisation elements (NCS score charts, sparklines) do not yet have full screen reader equivalents. We are working to add text alternatives in our next development cycle.

📬

Feedback and assistance

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or need assistance using any part of the platform, email support@sohana.app with the subject line 'Accessibility'. We will respond within 48 hours.
