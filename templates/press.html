04 · The story behind the platform

Why this is a story worth telling.

Sohana is developing a platform that digitises and scales community-based savings systems — ROSCAs — transforming them into structured, transparent, and globally accessible financial tools.

We believe the future of finance in emerging markets will not come solely from traditional institutions — but from strengthening systems that already work. We operate at the intersection of informal finance, digital infrastructure, and cross-border communities — three spaces that are rapidly evolving and still largely underreported.

"There are stories that are built for attention. And there are stories that quietly reshape systems. Sohana is the latter."
