Your savings behaviour is your credit score

The Njangi Credit Score (NCS) ranges from 300 to 850 and is calculated entirely from how you behave in savings circles — not from your bank balance or employment history.

📈

Five components, one score

Reliability (35%), cycle completion (25%), default recovery (20%), social trust (10%), and wallet behaviour (10%) combine into a single number that tells lenders everything they need to know.

🏆

19 achievement badges

From your first contribution to completing ten full circles, every savings milestone earns a badge permanently recorded on your public profile.

🚀

Unlock better financial products

An NCS of 550 unlocks emergency loans. 650 unlocks early payout options. 750 signals Exemplary tier — the highest recognition SOHANA awards.
