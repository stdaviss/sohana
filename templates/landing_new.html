01 · Everything your savings circle needs

The tontine your aunties run, with the rails of a modern bank.

Run weekly, biweekly or monthly circles in any of eight currencies. Transparent contributions, automated payouts, and full organiser controls — without a WhatsApp ledger in sight.

A · ORGANISER

Organiser controls, not group-chat chaos

Invite members by Hanatag, set the contribution rules, freeze a defaulter, rotate the payout slot — Sohana enforces what your circle agreed at the start.

B · TRANSPARENT FUNDS

Funds held in regulated escrow

Every contribution sits in a segregated, ringfenced account. Members see balances live; payouts auto-release on schedule with full audit trail.

C · FLEXIBLE

Weekly, biweekly, monthly

Run circles however your community runs them. 3 to 50 members. €20 to €5,000 contributions.

D · RECOVERY

Missed-contribution buffer

Optional shared buffer pool covers a missed round so payouts never stall. Repaid from the member's next contribution, automatically.

E · MULTI-CIRCLE

Run as many as you want

Family savings, work chama, holiday fund, baby fund. Each circle has its own rules, members and currency.
