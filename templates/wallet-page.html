01 · What the wallet is

A multi-currency account, designed for African and diaspora life.

Your Sohana wallet is the financial home for your money inside the platform. It is not a single account in a single currency — it is a multi-currency container that lets you hold balances in any of eight supported currencies side by side. EUR for your salary in Paris, GBP for the rent you receive in London, XAF for what you send home, NGN for what your cousin sends back. Each balance is real, segregated, and instantly available.

The wallet is more than storage. It is the financial engine that powers every other product on Sohana. When you join a Njangi circle, contributions move from your wallet. When a Pool admin approves a disbursement, funds land in the recipient's wallet. When someone sends a donation to a campaign, it leaves their wallet and arrives in the campaign escrow. When you pay a friend by Hanatag, your wallets handle the transfer in seconds.

The interface is built around the four operations you actually do: top up, withdraw, convert, pay. Every other action — contributing, donating, requesting, splitting — is a variation on those four primitives. Once you understand the wallet, you understand how money moves on Sohana.

Adding money to your wallet

You can fund your wallet using whichever method makes sense for the country you are in. Bank transfer is the default for users in EU, UK, US, and Canada — settle in one to two business days, no platform fee. Mobile money is available for users on the continent — instant settlement with provider fees passed through transparently.

You can also receive money instantly from any other Sohana user via their Hanatag — the simplest top-up, with zero fees and zero waiting. As long as the sender has a balance, your wallet receives it in seconds.

Sending money out of the wallet

When you want to move funds off the platform — to your bank account, your mobile money wallet, or another Sohana user — the wallet handles the routing. Withdrawals to EU bank accounts cost 1.0%, UK 1.5%, US 2.0%, and SWIFT (anywhere else) 3.5%. Mobile money disbursements are flat 1.5%. Sending to another Sohana user is always free.

Every fee is itemised on screen before you confirm. The minimum withdrawal fee is 50 cents — the floor for processing costs. There are no hidden charges, no surprise FX markups, no "convenience fees" added at the last screen.

Convert between any two of your Sohana wallets in seconds. The exchange rate shown is the live mid-market rate — the same rate banks use among themselves — with a single, transparent 0.7% conversion fee. No hidden spread, no opaque "we use our own rate" clause, no per-transaction surcharge.

Conversions are useful all the time. A diaspora user receives a salary in EUR, converts a portion to XAF to send home, keeps the rest in EUR. A parent in Lagos converts NGN to GBP to top up their student's UK wallet. A small business converts USD client payments to local currency. The wallet handles all of it without you ever leaving the app.

05 · Coming soon

Spend straight from your wallet, anywhere in the world.

In design · 2027

The Sohana debit card is coming.

Right now your wallet is for moving money inside the network — between friends, into circles, to family abroad. The next chapter is making that same balance spendable in the real world. We are building two card products that will sit on top of your existing wallet, with no separate account or top-up step required.

The virtual card launches first — issued instantly to your phone, ready for online checkout, in-app purchases, and tap-to-pay through Apple Pay and Google Pay. The physical card follows shortly after — a beautifully designed contactless card that draws directly from your multi-currency wallet, automatically picking the right currency for the merchant.

  • Multi-currency by default — the card detects the merchant's currency and spends from the matching wallet, falling back to your base currency with the standard 0.7% conversion if needed.
  • Real-time spending alerts — every transaction triggers an instant notification with full details, so you always know what's been spent.
  • Freeze instantly — lock and unlock the card from the app in one tap. No call centre, no waiting.
  • Spending controls — set per-category and per-merchant limits to keep your circle's pool funds protected from accidental overspend.
  • Built on a regulated card network — issued through a licensed partner, with the same consumer protections you expect from any major card.

07 · Security & limits

Built with guardrails by default.

Every wallet operates under sensible limits to protect users from mistakes, compromised devices, and fraud. Limits scale up with your KYC level — full verification unlocks higher daily and monthly thresholds. Beyond limits, the wallet is wrapped in the same security layer that protects every Sohana product: PBKDF2-hashed passwords, session controls, role-based access, and complete audit trails.
