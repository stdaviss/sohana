SOHANA Fundraising is the community-native alternative to GoFundMe — built for African diaspora communities who already trust each other, and want that trust to be transparent.

💚

Start a campaign in minutes

Create a public fundraising page for any cause — medical bills, school fees, funerals, community projects, or business startups. Every campaign gets a shareable link.

Full transparency for donors

Donors can see every contribution received, the total raised, and the goal progress in real time. No mystery. No middleman.

SOHANA members donate directly from their wallet balance — no card entry, no friction. Funds reach the campaign creator instantly after the 2.5% platform fee.

Every donation earns you NCS points. Generosity on SOHANA is not just a moral act — it is a verifiable financial behaviour that strengthens your credit identity.
