Understanding Your NCS Score

Your NCS score is the most important number in your SOHANA account. Here is exactly how it is calculated, what each tier means, and how to build it as fast as possible.

Get started free How it works
📊

How your score is calculated

Five weighted components determine your NCS: contribution reliability (35%), cycle completion (25%), default recovery (20%), peer endorsements (10%), and wallet behaviour (10%). The score updates in real time with every action.

📈

How to improve your score

Pay contributions on time. Complete full cycles without missing a payment. Endorse reliable members in your circles. Make regular deposits to your wallet. Help fellow members by covering their contributions when they are in difficulty.

🏅

NCS tiers and what they unlock

Probation (300–549): Basic access. Developing (550–649): Reduced creation fees. Reliable (650–749): Early payout options. Exemplary (750–850): Free circle creation, priority payout, and credit partner access.

⚠️

What lowers your score

Missing a contribution: -18 points. Late payment: -5 points. Being removed from a circle by an organiser: -8 points. Defaulting on a full cycle: -30 points.
