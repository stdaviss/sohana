SOHANA is an award-winning financial technology platform and proud recipient of the Tony Elumelu Foundation Entrepreneurship Award (TEF Cohort 2026). We are currently in active development and beta testing phase.

SOHANA is in the process of obtaining the necessary regulatory authorisations to operate as a licensed payment services provider. We are working towards registration with the relevant financial regulatory authorities in our operating jurisdictions, including the Autorité de Contrôle Prudentiel et de Résolution (ACPR) in France. Until such authorisations are obtained, SOHANA does not process real financial transactions and all platform activity remains in a controlled testing environment.

User data collected during the beta phase is handled in accordance with applicable data protection regulations. SOHANA does not hold or manage client funds at this stage of development.

For questions about our regulatory status or licensing roadmap, please contact legal@sohana.app.

SOHANA is a financial technology platform. Savings circle services and wallet operations are provided by SOHANA Technologies SAS, incorporated in France. SOHANA is registered with the Autorité de Contrôle Prudentiel et de Résolution (ACPR) as an agent of a licensed payment institution.

SOHANA does not hold client funds directly. All user balances are held in segregated accounts with licensed partner institutions. Your funds are protected in accordance with applicable payment services regulations.

Currency conversion and cross-border transfer services are subject to applicable exchange control regulations in each operating country. Contribution circle participation does not constitute a guaranteed investment or savings product. SOHANA complies with applicable Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations in all jurisdictions where it operates.
